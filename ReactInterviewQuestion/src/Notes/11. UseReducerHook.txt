1 What is useReducer in React?
    -   It is a hook used for managing complex state logic by utilizing a reducer function.
    -   Alternative to useState and provides a way to update states based on defined actions.

2. When to use useReducer instead of useState?
    -   When managing complex state transitions or logic that involves multiple sub-values.
    -   When state logic follows a pattern or when multiple actions need to update the state in predictable
        ways.
